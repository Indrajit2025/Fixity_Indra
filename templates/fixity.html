The Fixity Process

1. Report an Issue

Snap a photo or describe the problem. Our app automatically tags the location.

2. AI Analysis

Our AI categorizes the issue and assigns it to the correct municipal department instantly.

3. Authority Action

The assigned authority receives the report and updates its status as they work on it.

4. Resolution & Feedback

Once resolved, you get a notification. You can then provide feedback on the resolution.
